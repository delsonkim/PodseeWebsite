The Problem

Parents already have your centre in mind. The information stops them from booking.

When a parent is ready to act, they want to confirm three things fast: does the schedule work, does the price fit, and what is the trial policy. If they cannot find the answers immediately, the moment passes.

What parents want to confirm before booking
Does the trial slot carry over to regular classes?
What is the trial price vs monthly fee?
Who is the teacher and what is their background?
Which time slots are still available?
What is the replacement policy for missed lessons?
